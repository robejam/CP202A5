About Us

Providing delicious, fast, easy, and affordable meal recipes for young professionals and students.

StudentEats is a blog run by two Wilfrid Laurier University students that aim to supply healthy and affordable meal ideas to students. At StudentEats we believe that all students should be eating a full set of meals, that is why we supply students with four different meal selections: breakfast, lunch, dinner, and a snack option to be enjoyed whenever you feel like a nibble. Furthermore, At StudentEats we aim to please all audiences with our food ideas. That is why we have produced vegetarian options in all of our sections. We hope you enjoy the meals that are currently offered and can promise you that we look to expand to more great meal ideas in the near future.
