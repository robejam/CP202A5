Nutritious Snack

Perfect For Any Student On The Go

Posted by Nathan Roberts on November 21, 2017

Hummus cheese and carrot stick with cheese and apple. Clean, easy, fast and effective. A cheap, quick meal that is packed with nutrients. this snack is perfect for any student running out the door before a busy class schedule.

Things You Need

  • 1 Carrot peeled and sliced
  • 1 Portion of store purchased hummus
  • 2 Inch cube of cheese
  • 1 Apple cored and sliced

If you want you can try to make your own hummus, its easier then you think if you have a food processor or blender kicking around.

Step 1

Peel carrots and slice them to bite-sized pieces.

Step 2

Arrange all ingredients on plate, place hummus in small container for dipping goodness
