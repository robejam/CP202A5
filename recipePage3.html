Tuna and Mayonnaise Sandwich

Easy, Effective, and Portable

Posted by Nathan Roberts on November 25, 2017

No its not the latest iPhone, this is a tuna and mayonnaise sandwich. It is an easy and effective way to create a healthy option that can be prepared at home and taken on the road. This meal covers all the nutrients you need in carbohydrates, proteins and fats.

Things You Need

  • 2 slices of bread or 1 bun
  • 1 tablespoon of mayo
  • 1 can of tuna
  • 1 portion hot sauce

Lots of different flavour choices of mayonnaise

Step 1

Mix mayo, hot sauce and tuna in container

Step 2

Spread the mixture onto sliced bread

Step 3

Combine all ingredients into a sandwich. Enjoy cold.
