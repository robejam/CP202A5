Almond Butter Banana Wrap

Great Bedtime Snack

Posted by Nathan Roberts on November 20, 2017

this meal is a great bedtime snack or a good option for a meal to eat on the meal. This snack is guaranteed to keep you fueled with a sticky mouthful of delicious food.

Things You Need

  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Whole grain tortillas
  • 1 Tablespoon almond butter

If you have a chocolate craving swap the almond butter for nutella.
Another fun choice is to use crêpes and not tortillas. Less healthy, but ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

Step 1

Peel and slice banana

Step 2

Spread almond butter onto wholegrain tortillas

Step 3

Arrange banana slice on top of almond butter

Step 4

Roll wrap around contents and enjoy.
