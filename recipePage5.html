Ground Beef On Rice

Full Of Macro Nutrients

Posted by Nathan Roberts on November 23, 2017

A cheap and fast meals for students, ground beef on rice creates a filling delicious meal that can be prepped on the tightest of schedules. This meal comes packed with different nutrients and is full of your macro nutrients to ensure that you have the energy to fuel your body.

Things You Need

  • 1 Pound lean ground beef
  • 1 Teaspoon salt
  • 1 Cup beef broth
  • 1 Can of peas
  • 1 Cup of mix vegetables
  • 1 & 1/2 cups instant brown rice, uncooked

Step 1

Preheat skillet to medium temperature.

Step 2

Add grown beef and cook till brown.

Step 3

Add beef broth and salt to pan.

Step 4

Cook for 15 minutes.

Step 5

Add peas and corn to mixture.

Step 6

Place rice in microwave and cook according to instructions dependant on microwave and brand.

Step 7

Mix thoroughly in pan.
