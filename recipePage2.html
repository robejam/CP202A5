Protein Pancakes

Healthy Pancakes Anyone?

Posted by Nathan Roberts on November 26, 2017

Healthy pancakes? Yes, it is possible. These pancakes deliver the delicious taste of pancakes without the heavy overdose of simple carbohydrates. a full stack of protein-infused pancakes creates great taste with outstanding nutrients.

Things You Need

  • 1 scoop of protein powder
  • 1 cup of oatmeal
  • 1 egg 2 egg whites
  • Â½ tsp baking soda

You can use different flavors of protein powder to give them a diffent flavor profile.

Step 1

Mix all ingredients in a small bowl.

Step 2

Pour onto heated pan or griddle.

Step 3

Cook until golden brown, approximately 2 to 3 minutes per side.

Step 3

If you wish, Garnish with fruit of your choice
